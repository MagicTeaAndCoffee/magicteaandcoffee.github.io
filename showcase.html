Strawberry Matcha Latte

The latte is a delightful and refreshing twist on the classic boba tea. This creamy and indulgent beverage combines the rich flavors of smooth vanilla and luscious strawberries with the comforting warmth of freshly brewed espresso. Served over ice, this drink is not only visually appealing with its vibrant pink hue but also a treat for the taste buds. Each sip delivers a balanced blend of sweetness from the strawberries and a hint of floral notes from the vanilla, creating a harmonious and satisfying beverage. Whether you're a boba enthusiast or a coffee lover, the vanilla strawberry latte boba is sure to be a delightful choice that will leave you craving more.
